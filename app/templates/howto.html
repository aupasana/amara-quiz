How to use this site

This site is designed for those who want to learn and use the amarakosha in a traditional way. i.e It it centered around the verses of the amarakosha themselves in their original context, and not merely it's vocabulary.
Search the amarakosha
Enter a search term into the search box on the top of every page. The search term should be in itrans or devanagari format. Type enter or click the search icon to the right side of the search box.
Search the apte sanskrit-english dictionary
Enter a search term into the search box on the top of every page. The search term should be in itrans or devanagari format. Type shift-enter or click the "A" icon to the right side of the search box.
